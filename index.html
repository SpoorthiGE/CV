About Me

My name is Spoorthi GE, and I am a highly skilled professional with a strong educational background and extensive experience in the fields of Image Processing, Machine Learning, and Software Engineering. I am excited to introduce myself and highlight some key aspects of my academic achievements and professional journey.

I have actively contributed to the field through my patents and publications. I have filed two patent applications on applying machine learning for advanced acoustic technology, awaiting approval from the USPTO. Furthermore, I have published several research papers in esteemed journals and conferences. Notably, my work on "PhaseNet 2.0: A Deep Learning Approach for Unwrapping the Phase from Noisy Data" was published in IEEE Transactions on Image Processing. Another significant publication was "PhaseNet: A Deep Convolutional Neural Network for Two-Dimensional Phase Unwrapping," featured in IEEE Signal Processing Letters as one of the top 25 popular articles.

Moving on to my professional experience, I am currently serving as a Senior Software Engineer II at Harman International Pvt Ltd, Harman X (R&D) Bangalore, since May 2022. In this role, I have been instrumental in the research, design, and productization of acoustic technology solutions utilizing machine learning. My recent focus has been on optimizing deep learning networks, encompassing problem formulation, feature selection, data generation, training, hyper-parameter tuning, and network optimization with quantization techniques.

Prior to my current position, I worked as a Research and Development Engineer at K2O Consulting Pvt Ltd Bangalore from February 2020 to April 2022. During my tenure, I specialized in developing algorithms and tools for obtaining precise tooth profiles using intraoral scanners. I successfully devised an algorithm for obtaining depth maps from structured light techniques and developed pre-processing and post-processing tools for accurate registration and visualization of 3D models.

My interests include Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Neuroscience and Signal Processing.
