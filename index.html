About Me

I am a Software Engineer (R & D) at K2O, where I work on developing high-speed and accurate intra-oral scanners. I have contributed in obtaining precise depth maps from structured light techniques.

I have pursued my Masters' (MS(by Research)) in Image Processing and Machine Learning from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Tirupati, India. During this period, I have proposed a novel deep learning-based phase unwrapping technique. This was one of the first research works to use the deep learning technique for unwrapping the phase. My research work is published in IEEE Signal Processing Letters and IEEE Transactions on Image Processing. I did my Bachelors (B.E.) in Electronics and communication, from RNS Institute of Technology, India. I have also worked in the industry after my bachelor's for two and half years that honed my communication and coding skills.

My interests include Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Neuroscience and Signal Processing.
